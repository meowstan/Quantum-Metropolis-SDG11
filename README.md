# Quantum Metropolis: SDG 11

**Developer:** Ilkin Gasimov
**Theme:** SDG 11 - Sustainable Cities and Communities
**Engine:** Godot (2D)

## About the Project
"Quantum Metropolis" is a grid-based puzzle-strategy game where the player acts as a City Mayor managing urban development. The game uses quantum mechanics (Superposition, Measurement/Collapse, and Entanglement) to simulate the unpredictable nature of urban crises like pollution, traffic congestion, and housing shortages.

## Core Quantum Mechanics
* **Superposition:** Unresolved city districts exist in a superposition of states (e.g., both "Developing" and "Decaying") until an "Audit" is performed.
* **Measurement & Collapse:** Performing an "Audit" collapses the district's state into a definitive positive or negative outcome.
* **Entanglement:** Certain city districts are linked; an action performed in District A instantly alters the probability state of District B.
